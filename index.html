History & Identity

🏛️ Origins (1940)

Founded in 1940, the school site originally served as a hospital for 25 years. Named after British Army officer Allan Wilson, it was accorded "technical high school" status in 1952, establishing a long tradition of practical and academic rigor.

📍 Location & Neighbors

Allan Wilson is located in Harare, Zimbabwe. It shares a boundary fence with Prince Edward School to the north and Harare Polytechnic to the south.

📍 17.77015°S 31.01076°E

🛠️ Technical Evolution

Originally academically selective with a technical/scientific curriculum, the school broadened its scope in 1974. AWS places a strong emphasis on industrial arts, offering hands-on experience in metalwork, woodwork, and technical drawing.

🤝 Sister School

Our sister school is Queen Elizabeth Girls' High School, which was founded in the same year, 1940.

Academic Departments

📚 Curriculum

Since 1988, the school has followed the ZIMSEC protocol. O-Levels are written at the end of Form IV, and A-Levels at the end of Form VI.

We offer a comprehensive range of subjects from Sciences to Commercials, Arts, and Technical disciplines.
